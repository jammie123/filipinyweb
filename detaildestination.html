BORACAY ISLAND

If you are one of the party lovers, your attention can not escape the island of Boracay. Boracay is one of the smaller islands of the Philippines, but one of the most well-known and most visited.

More photo

How to get there

The fastest way to get to this small paradise is to use AirAsia, Philippine Airlines or Cebu Pacific. On the island of Boracay you will not find the airport, but fortunately near the island there are two airports. The first airport is located in Caticlan, which is approximately 15 minutes by boat from Boracay. The second airport is located in Kalibo, where it takes about 2 hours to take you to this island.

ACTIVITIES ON THE ISLAND

Boracay is not only famous for its party. On the island you will find plenty of other activities. Start on the water. From the all-day Island hopping tour, where you look at other smaller islands near Boracay to various water activities. In this pristine water you can try diving, paddleboat, parasailing, or by sailing the traditional way using the infamous Paraw to capture the wonderful sunset and enjoy refreshing drinks. Not to mention that there is also a part of Boracay that is very windy so lovers of windsurfing and kiting can show off their art. There is also a training lesson for beginners. A little romance can be enjoyed on a horseback riding at sunset.
